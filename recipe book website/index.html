Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Preparation Time 20 minutes
Cooking Time 45 minutes
Serves 4 people

Ingridients

  • 500g spaghetti
  • 500g ground chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat some oil and saut√© the onion and garlic until soft
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve the sauce over the spaghetti
